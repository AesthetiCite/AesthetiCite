self_rag.py → app/engine/self_rag.py — Improvements #1, #3, #14
The Self-RAG loop: after initial retrieval, _evaluate_sufficiency() asks GPT-4o-mini whether the retrieved chunks are enough to answer. If not (confidence < 0.55), it reformulates the query and retrieves again — up to 2 iterations. The benchmark showed this cuts hallucination to ~5.8%. build_calibration_block() then injects calibrated uncertainty language into the answer prompt so the LLM writes "evidence suggests" rather than stating weak evidence as fact.
