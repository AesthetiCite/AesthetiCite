Here's the full picture of what was built and why the structure is exactly this way.

The screen
Dark slate-950 background — not a white card stack. Every other clinical tool looks like a hospital admin system. This looks like something designed to be used with urgency.
The sticky header shows the protocol name and urgency badge (IMMEDIATE pulsing red) at all times regardless of scroll position. The reset button (↺) is always accessible. During a vascular occlusion at 14:30, the clinician should never have to scroll back to the top to start over.
The 6-section order is deliberate
Every other platform puts evidence first because it's an evidence platform. AesthetiCite puts actions first because the clinical need is actions. Section 5 (Evidence) is collapsed by default. During a live complication you do not need citations — you need the next step. Evidence is there when you want to check your reasoning or you're in a non-urgent situation.
