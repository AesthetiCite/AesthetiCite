The component has three problems before touching the design:
Wrong endpoint — it calls /api/safety/v2/protocol which doesn't exist in your backend. The real endpoint is POST /api/complications/protocol. That call will 404 on every demo.
Wrong schema — your ProtocolRequest expects a query string plus a ClinicalContext object. The component sends flat fields that won't map.
Missing half the output — ProtocolResponse returns risk_assessment, dose_guidance, red_flags, escalation, follow_up_questions, limitations, and disclaimer. None of those are rendered. Those are clinically the most important fields.
